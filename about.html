Hi, I'm Michelle.

A UCSD alumna with a Bachelor's in Human-Computer Interaction and a minor in Computer Science, I've spent the last 5 years doing what I genuinely love — designing. My background bridges the gap between human empathy and technical precision, which means I don't just make things look good, I make them work beautifully for the people using them.

Over the years I've worked across product design, UX research, and visual design — always with the same north star: create experiences that feel effortless and look intentional. I believe great design is invisible; it guides you without you noticing.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me on the ice rink, behind a camera lens, or deep in a rabbit hole of interior design inspiration. Design isn't just my job — it's the lens through which I see everything.
